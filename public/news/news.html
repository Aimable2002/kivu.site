Kigali named top destination for tech conferences in 2026

KIGALI, RWANDA — The Rwanda Convention Bureau announced today that the city will host three major pan-African summits next year, bringing thousands of delegates to the capital.

This milestone marks a significant achievement for the country's strategic shift towards becoming the "Silicon Valley of Africa." Investments in digital infrastructure, high-speed internet, and world-class venues like the Kigali Convention Centre have paved the way for this success.

Local businesses, especially in the hospitality and transport sectors, are expecting a massive boost in revenue. "This is exactly what our economy needs right now," said a local hotel manager.
